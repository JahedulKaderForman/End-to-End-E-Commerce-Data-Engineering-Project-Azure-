E-Commerce Sales & Analytics Dashboard 

Measure :

Total Sales = SUM(fact_order[total_price])

Total Orders = DISTINCTCOUNT(fact_order[order_id])

Total Customers = DISTINCTCOUNT(fact_order[customer_key])

Average Order Value = DIVIDE([Total Sales], [Total Orders])


KPI Cards :
 Total Sales
 Total Orders
 Total Customers
 Average Order Value


Sales Trend Over Time (Line chart) :
  dim_date[date] → X-axis
  Total Sales → Y-axis


Revenue by Product Category (Clustered column chart ) :
  Total Sales  → X-axis
  dim_product[category] → Y-axis



Top Products (Bar chart) :
 dim_product[product_name] → Y-axis
 Total Sales → X-axis



Customers by Country (Stacked column chart or a Map) :
dim_customer[country] → Axis / Location
Total Sales or Total Orders → Values


Orders by Order Status (PIE CHART) :
fact_order[order_status]  -> Legend
Total Orders ->  Values


Sales by Month  ( DONUT CHART ) :
dim_date[month] -> Legend
Total Sales -> Values


Add Slicers (Filters) :
slicer: dim_product[category]
Another : dim_customer[country]
